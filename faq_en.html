What is DanceAR App?

With the DanceAR App you can experience 6 dance choreographies of Gauthier Dance up close and in the middle / location-independent. You can bring the performances to life and play them back in your room or on one of our markers (coasters/postcards). In addition, you can take a photo or film of your live camera with the record button.

For the best experience, we recommend downloading the movies for offline use/viewing.

If you are streaming, please keep in mind that the total data size is about 2 GB.

Which operating systems are supported?

The DanceAR app is available for iOS (Apple) and Android for free in the App and Play Store. However, the app can only be used if the device used supports augmented reality. (alternatively) DanceAR App is a free AR app for iOS and Android. It can be installed and used for free on both Apple iOS and Android smartphones and tablets.

On which devices can I use the DanceAR app? / Does my device support augmented reality?

To use the DanceAR app, a device that supports augmented reality (or ARCore for Android devices and ARKit for Apple devices) is required. A list of all AR-enabled devices can be found here: https://developers.google.com/ar/discover/supported-devices
